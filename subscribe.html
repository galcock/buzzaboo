Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time from the billing page. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), debit cards, and various digital wallets through Stripe.
Do subscriptions auto-renew?
Yes, subscriptions automatically renew monthly unless you cancel. You'll receive an email reminder before each renewal.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Absolutely! You can change your subscription tier at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated difference immediately. When downgrading, the change takes effect at your next billing cycle.
Is there a free trial?
We currently don't offer free trials, but you can always use Buzzaboo for free! Paid subscriptions unlock additional features but aren't required to enjoy the platform.
What happens if my payment fails?
If a payment fails, we'll retry a few times over the next few days and send you email notifications. If payment continues to fail, your subscription will be paused until you update your payment method.
